Hello! I'm Liam, I am currently an Honours student in mathematics at the Australian National University in Canberra. I am jointly supervised by [Svetlana Markarova](https://sites.google.com/view/murmuno/main-page?authuser=0) and [Anand Deopurkar](https://ananddeopurkar.org). Broadly speaking, my mathematical interests lie in the intersection of Algebraic Geometry, Homotopy Theory, and Representation Theory. Currently I am thinking about \\(\mathbb{A}^1\\) homotopy theory, and in particular the quadratically enriched enumerative geometry program of Levine-Wicklegren-et. al. 

My general taste in mathematics is in the application of geometric methods to study objects arising in algebraic contexts. Put simply, I enjoy algebra that features pretty pictures.
